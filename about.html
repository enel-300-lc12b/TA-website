About

Our Pursuit

We're a group of second year electrical engineering students with a goal. Our mission is to help plant owners take the best care of their plants with as little hassle as possible. We've been victims of forgetfulness far too many times; countless numbers of our plants have ended up suffering due to our neglect for them, and we're sure many other plants undergo the same thing.

Innovative Plant Care

The TA (Temperature Alarm) is our solution to this problem. It is a plant monitoring device that can monitor the temperature and soil moisture content, alerting the user if either of these parameters are undesirable for the plant. The wireless speaker allows for instant notification even from a distance away. The user can set a range of temperatures to stay within for cusomization, and the modular temperature and soil sensors makes repair easy. With the TA, having healthy plants is as easy as ever.
