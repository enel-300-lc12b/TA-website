We have a plan.

We know sustainability is important to you, and it's important to us too. Earth only has so many resources to go around after all. That's why we've designed the TA with sustainability in mind.

Modularity

The TA is modular in the sense that it has multiple additional components that will add on top of the temperature sensor. Users are free to pick the additional components along with the standalone product. For example, a soil moisture detector can be purchased separately from the thermometer.


This allows you to only acquire the parts of the product that you need, and replacing parts when they wear out is no problem. This reduces the waste produced from our device. Modularity also works well with design for disassembly, which is another sustainability consideration we took into account; with a modular unit, the case can come off to be recycled.

Design for Disassembly

Having a modular product also means that it can be disassembled when the time comes. Components for the TA can easily be disassembled when repairs are needed. The casing can come off when it needs to be recycled, and the moisture sensor can be removed when it is not in use or damaged.


Displosal for disassembly allows for longevity of the product because each component can be replaced separately. This makes it easy to recycle part of the product at the end of its life by separating the recyclable casing from the rest of the device, reducing waste and allowing you to dispose of the prodict in an environmentally friendly way.

Environmentally Friendly Materials

We use sustainable materials for the casing of the product. The plastic used for the casing is recyclable and comes from renewable resources. We support ethical sourcing of our products and aim for a carbon neutral approach to production.


We're committed to minimizing our carbon footprint. Using materials that are recyclable or biodegradable is one step towards this. Providing a high quality and modular product will prevent waste from going into landfills, and when the product does reach the end of its life, parts of it are recycled to extend the life of the materials used.

Production on Demand

Our product is made only after your order has been placed. Production on demand helps to reduce the amount of waste produced. Since units are only made when users order them, there will not be leftovers that go unsold.

Repair

A repair manual will be provided with the purchase of the product. The repair manual will list all the components and give clear instructions on how to repair and replace them. Users will be able to purchase components that are broken to replace.


This reduces waste because you only buy the parts you need, rather than a whole new product. Since the product is modular, if a specific component breaks, users can simply replace it using the repair manual.

E-Shop

The very website you're browsing right now provices the platform for us to implement our production on demand and modular models. Customers are free to shop for what they need online from the comfort of their own home. Our website is also used to help promote transparency regarding our products and manufacturing.
